Development as Collaboration

Global aid is facing a major shift toward community-centric programs. With more engaged communities, and the rise of mobile technology, aid delivery requires unprecedented collaboration between

developing and developed countries. Organizations making this adaptation will be more effective in the short term and more responsive to development needs and opportunities in the long term.
